About Me

My name is Michael Anthony McGhee Jr. I was born and raised in Richmond Virginia my whole life. I attended High School at Hanover High School and Lee Davis High School (for Navy ROTC) in Mechanicsville, VA. After Graduation I attended VCU School of Political Science for a year before entering the US Navy in 2019. Upon Medical separation, later that year, I found a new love for Electrical work in the Union; IBEW Local #666.


In the Union, I built Datacenters for Facebook/META for 3 years as an Apprentice Electrician. Soon thereafter I found a position at Family Electric, my current role, as a Foreman and have been running jobs with them ever since. My current role as an electrician is fulfilling, however, not as much of a challenge for my brain as I would like in my professional career. So I sought out Computer Programming/ Software Development with the University of Richmond. I plan to start a new, much more fulfilling, career as a developer to expand my horizons and lifestyle. Nothing has had me so focused and ready to put in the work as this program and I am so excited to see it through.
